Tribute to Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj (1630–1680) was the founder of the Maratha Empire. He was a great warrior, king, and administrator who fought for Swarajya (self-rule). He is a pride of Maharashtra and all of India.

Achievements

Founded the Maratha Empire.

Father of the Indian Navy.

Built and captured many forts.

Defeated Afzal Khan at Battle of Pratapgad.
